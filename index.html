WEEK ONE

Getting Started

Day one was all about settling in and getting myself used to the new surroundings. I was tasked with creating three icons. An exercise in throwing myself back into the arms of Illustrator. I had gotten used to working with on Sketch that it was a little daunting in the beginning.

The Icons were for different sections of the company Food Coasts' website. Network and Experiences, News and Events and Certified Member. With all design tasks, I got straight to researching. The company's original Logo is Circular with solid icons, so I took to Tumblr, Pinterest, and Dribbble in search of inspiration. I found a few icons I liked that were circular and had clean line art illustrations.
